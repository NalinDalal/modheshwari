# Modheshwari Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose

At **Modheshwari**, we are dedicated to providing a welcoming, safe, and inclusive community. This code of conduct outlines our expectations for all participants, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## 2. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by **Modheshwari**, including public and private repositories, communication channels, and social media accounts. It also applies to any events or activities related to the project, both online and offline.

## 3. Expected Behavior

We expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and considerate in their communication.
- Foster an inclusive environment, where individuals from diverse backgrounds feel valued.
- Provide constructive feedback and collaborate positively.
- Refrain from discriminatory, offensive, or inappropriate language or behavior.
- Be open to questions and discussions without hostility.

## 4. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:
- Harassment, intimidation, or exclusion based on gender, gender identity, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, ethnicity, religion, or other personal characteristics.
- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive or aggressive communication, including trolling or deliberate misrepresentation of arguments.
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Any form of cyberbullying or doxxing (sharing personal information without consent).
- Plagiarizing or misrepresenting work done by others.

## 5. Reporting Guidelines

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the **Modheshwari** team at [email contact] immediately. We will investigate and take appropriate action. All reports will remain confidential.

## 6. Consequences

Participants who violate this Code of Conduct may face actions such as:
- A warning or temporary ban from the project or community spaces.
- Permanent removal from the community.
- Reporting to relevant authorities if the behavior is illegal or threatening.

## 7. Acknowledgments

This Code of Conduct is inspired by [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), which provides guidelines for building welcoming and respectful open-source communities.
